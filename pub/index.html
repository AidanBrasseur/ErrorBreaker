Waiting around has never been more fun

Have you ever found yourself waiting around on a long loading screen or staring blankly at a meaningless error code? You are certainly not alone, and ErrorBreaker hopes to fill that empty space with some equally mindless classic gaming.

ErrorBreaker generates a flexible Brick Breaker game on the page, giving developers the opportunity to provide their users with a more fun and engaging way to interact with loading or error information. The level will take the shape of whatever alphanumeric text fits your needs and an optional built in progress bar can also be used! Customize the colours, text, and even audio to your liking!
